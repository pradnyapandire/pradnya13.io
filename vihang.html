About Vihang's Inn

Designed in an international style, the contemporary décor of Vihang's Inn, Thane, Mumbai, extends to its elegant event venues as well. If you've got a smaller function on the cards, like a sangeet or mehendi, the rooftop Orchid will give you a gorgeous open-air venue. The silver-grey sparkle of Z+ works for a glamorous wedding cocktail or youngsters' party. Savour rich Punjabi and other delicacies as part of your buffet, and let Bihang's Inn arrange your décor as well. Venue does not allow outside caterers. Venue does not allow outside decorators. In-house décor provided by Rising Star and Event Rider. Venue does not allow outside DJ. Venue allows outside alcohol. Corkage applicable.
