About Novotel Mumbai Juhu Beach

If you're planning a dusk wedding, you could have a breathtaking one at Novotel, Juhu, Mumbai. The hotel has a gorgeous poolside lawn area that looks out to the Arabian Sea. The sunset view from this lawn is known to be spectacular. Imagine taking your wedding vows against the backdrop of a sky painted in shades of pinks, oranges and violets. There's even a separate entry available at the venue for baraatis. Prefer to have an indoor function? The Champagne Ballroom at Novotel combines the best of modern technology, contemporary décor and classic luxury to give you an unforgettable experience. Their banquet hall and their pool side party area are sure to spoil you for choice!
