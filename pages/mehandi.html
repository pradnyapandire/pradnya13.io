In the world top four mehendi designs are much famous in all over world like they are Arabic mehendi design, Indian mehendi design, African mehendi designs and Pakistani mehendi design. These types of easy mehendi styles are best suited as well as perfect for wedding ceremony features, official occasions as well as celebrations. These types of latest mehendi design tend to be with regard to both of your hands as well as perfect for all ages for ladies. Females believe that mehendi is really a short term skin icon. Females used mehendi on the fingers as well as fit. It is extremely well known within Pakistan, Indian and Bangladesh along with other Arabic nations.
Indian Mehandi
Indian mehendi are the most popular designs that are very diverse. They range from simple designs to complex. Simple designs have a big dot or a figure in back of hand or in the center of palm. Figure tips are also covered by henna. This design is very easy and can easily be mastered by armatures
Pakistani Mehandi Design
Pakistani mehendi designs are a merger of complex versions of Arabic and Indian mehendi designs. These designs are also much detailed and are worn by brides on mehendi, wedding. Even children wear simple version of these mehendi designs on Eid festival.

Bridal Mehandi Design
These are mostly filled with number of different motifs, flowers and other patterns the top to the bottom.They start from the tips of the fingers and extent up to the half of the arms with different patterns in a flowing manner. The different lines, curves and dots add beauty to the pattern. Brides in India and Asian countries love have this style of mehndi.
Indo Arabic Design
The bold Arabic style is outline that is filled in with delicate. The traditional Indian patterns and shapes can be drawn in which as you can re-modify them. That is the most sought after style for Indian weddings.
African Design
Just like the Arabic counterparts these mehendi designs are also not filled. These designs are simple geometric shapes, consisting on lines, square and dots. The space between the lines is not as much as that of Arabic designs. These designs cover your hands, arms like a dine mesh.
Bamgle Style Mehandi Design
This type of style is made in bangle style. That round and round gives a beautiful look to your hands as well as legs. This style make you not to be tensed in selection of bangles. As it is shown in mehendi design only. So, here is a beautiful design with different color and makes you look beautiful and color full hands.
Rajastani Mehandi
It Comprise of complicated patterns with peacocks, flowers and several curves and curls in between without leaving much clear space. They are mostly done in fine lines covering the hands from the tips of the fingers to the elbows and the design on the feet extending from the toes to the knees.
Glitter Mehandi
This is the combinations of Indian and Arabic designs. They are bold designs with outlines filled with delicate patterns and different shapes. The designs are accentuated with sparkles. It has becomes the latest trend in the mehndi designs. The outlines of the design are studded with colourful stones of different colours.
Mughlai Mehandi
Here the designs are very neat and detailed. They have a distinctive style where every curl and dot is made prominent. Mughlai is the oldest and most traditional form of designs. It contains floral and geometric designs which are spread over the hands with some open spaces. Normally they extend from the tips of the finger to wrist in a slanting pattern comprising of flowers, leaves and petal filled with intrinsic lines and dots.
Arabic Mehandi
Compared with the complex Indian mehendi designs, Arabic designs are much simpler. Arabic designs mostly consist of leaves, veins, flowers etc. These designs are perfect candidate if you want to extend the design from hand to arm or from foot to leg. Another distinguishing feature of Arabic designs is that the shapes are not filled, they are just outlines. These designs also partially cover your hands or feet. These designs consume less amount of mehendi and are easily dried which means that you don’t have to wait!
