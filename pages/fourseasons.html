Four Seasons, Worli, Mumbai



About Four Seasons

 The Four Seasons, Worli, Mumbai, is dedicated to offering personalized services, maintaining a beautiful elegant setting and availing superior culinary experience to their customers. This continues in their banqueting and event services. Five luxurious banquet halls and an outdoor lawn can make any dream event transform into reality. Palatial and stunning, The Gallery is a long room (that can be divided into two sections for more intimate functions) outfitted with etched mirrors, linear pendant Czech chandeliers, dark wood doors and floor-to-ceiling windows that look out on to the lush and impeccably-tended lawn. It's the ideal setting for wedding pheras or other traditional ceremonies. There is also the Living Room, Library, Think Tank and Reading room for other occasions. All of the rooms are bathed in natural light (automated blackout shutters are available for projector meetings), have wood-panelled walls and hand-tufted wool carpets. Four Seasons Hotel also provides exclusive wedding planing services.
